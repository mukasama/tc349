Board of Directors

ABC Famers is managed by the following board of directors who bring the relevant skills and knowledge that they have acquired through school and work experience and to help the farmers in Uganda.

Syilvia Irumba - President

After graduating with a degree in agriculture and forest from University of Dar es Salaam in Tanzania. Syilvia went on to do her masters in Economics at the London Business School in London. Upon completion she joined, the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) as a representative for Uganda where she has acquired a lot of skills and networking techniques to help Ugandans.

Other Skills:

Group management, Savings and financial management, Basic business and marketing, Natural resource management for sustainable production.


Martin Mukasa - Director

Martin Mukasa is a recent graduate from Michigan State University with a degree in Media and Information with a minor in Information. He has conducted research for MSU in plant biology, physiology, production, yield and management of crops.

Other Skills:

Technology and innovation, Administration and Management, Personnel and Human Resources.
